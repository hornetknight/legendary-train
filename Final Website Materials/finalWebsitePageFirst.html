Why is sustainable development important for us?

Example of making use of space

To begin with sustainable building, it is making use of as much space as possible when designing buildings, as well as introducing as much green space as possible. What these two do is make quality of life more comfortable as well as better for the environment.

How does sustainable development help the lives of those in Third-World countries?

Sustainable development helps lives in third world countries by mitigating the impacts of natural disasters, preventing a heavy reliance on lots of electricity, and more options for citizens to grow their own food
